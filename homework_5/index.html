Popular marvel movies

Avengers

Thor's half-brother Loki is back and this time he is not alone. The Earth is on the verge of enslavement, and only the best of the best can save the mankind. Nick Fury, the director of the S.H.I.E.L.D. organization, gathers everyone to repel the attack. Under the leadership of Captain America, the Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Hawkeye and Black Widow enter the war with the invader.

Avengers: age of ultron

Humanity is on the brink of destruction. Ultron, the peacekeeping program is threatening the mankind. The S.H.I.E.L.D. Organization broke up, and the world is not able to cope with such a powerful enemy. So the people once again turn to the Earth's greatest heroes, the Avengers. However, Ultron is too powerful and there is a high probability that the Avengers will not manage to stop the age of Ultron, where there is no place for people…

Avengers: infinity war

A new threat looms over the world. Mighty villain Thanos intends to change the reality the way he wants, and he needs all six Infinity Stones to do this. The Avengers will have to come together again to save the Earth. But will they have enough strength this time, because the opponent has never been so strong and dangerous before?

Avengers: endgame

After the last unsuccessful fight, the surviving members of the Avengers team, along with allies, are going to come up with a new and unique plan, which will help the heroes to resist the destructive power of the evil Titan Thanos. They are waiting for the final battle, in which they will have only one chance to win.

Iron man

A billionaire inventor Tony Stark is captured by Afghan terrorists, who are trying to make him create a weapon of mass destruction. Tony secretly designs a high-tech cyber suit, with the help of which he successfully escapes. As he returns to the US, he learns that the board of directors of his company is weaving a plot, which might have disastrous consequences. Stark uses his latest invention to solve the problems of his company…

Thor

Due to his reckless behaviour, the powerful but arrogant god Thor is cast out of Asgard to live amongst humans on Earth, where he soon becomes one of their finest defenders.

Captain america: the first avenger

Steve Rogers volunteers to take part in an experiment that transforms him into a super soldier knows as Captain America. Together with Bucky Barnes and Peggy Carter he joins US forces to fight against the HYDRA organization, ruled by the ruthless Red Skull.

Guardians of the galaxy

American pilot is caught up in an intergalactic conflict and goes on the run with a group of futuristic prisoners, who has something for which everyone is hunting.

Ant man

Armed with a super-suit with the astonishing ability to shrink in scale but increase in strength, cat burglar Scott Lang must embrace his inner hero and help his mentor, Dr. Hank Pym, plan and pull off a heist that will save the world.

Doctor strange

Stephen Strange was a skilled and extremely self-confident surgeon. He adored his work. But a terrible accident takes his wife's life and makes his arm insensitive. Having lost the meaning of his life, he goes in search of means of healing his body and soul. Years later he becomes one of the most powerful sorcerers of the Earth.

Deadpool

Wade Wilson solves problems for money in a not very legal way. After he learns he is dying of cancer, he agree to participate in a secret program that will release his mutated genes. Now Wade is the invincible super hero, who is able to recover from any injury. Before returning to his girlfriend, Wilson turns the entire criminal world upside down to pull in a favour...

Venom

Venom is one of the most prominent antagonists in the world of comics. He is famous for being the sworn enemy of Spider-Man, who has defeated him in a battle for a couple of times. The whole separate project is dedicated to the hero, telling how he has turned into the villain.

Spiderman: homecoming

Peter Parker is just a student who gains superpowers in an accident in a laboratory. He is not prepared for such a role as the Avengers do. But only Parker will be able to save the city from the insidious villain who wants to deal destruction and get absolute power.

Spiderman: far from home

After the last major battle, Peter Parker finally manages to take a little vacation. Together with friends, he goes to Europe. However, soon his plans are crumbling — he has to help Nick Fury to stop creatures that wreak havoc around the world.

Spiderman: no way home

Peter Parker's ordinary life, as well as his reputation, are in danger as Mysterio reveals to the world who is really hiding behind the mask of Spider-Man. The guy decides to turn to Stephen Strange in the hope that he can somehow help him fix it. However, very soon the situation becomes even more dangerous...
